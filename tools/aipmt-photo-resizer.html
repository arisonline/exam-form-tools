What is All India Pre-medical Test (AIPMT)?

AIPMT is an Entrance Test for selection of candidates for admission to MBBS/BDS courses under 15% All India Quota seats in Government Medical colleges of India.

Except: Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Jammu & Kashmir States.

Is AIPMT & NEET are same?

No, AIPMT and NEET are not the same. NEET has replaced AIPMT (All India Pre-Medical test/ Pre-Dental Entrance Test) completely in the year 2017.

Now, NEET is the entrance exam for all medical and dental programs across India.

Basics tips for Act Test Exam Photo Requirements

Photo must be a clear image of only you against a plain background (not blurry, grainy, fuzzy).

The photo must be show your full head and shoulders shot, squarely facing the camera.

Your photo cannot be enhanced in any way. For example: no lenses, no dark glasses, filters, text, emoji, stickers, etc..

Photo format and sizes for AIPMT Test

  • Only JPG or JPEG image file accepted.
  • The photo must be at least 200 x 300 pixels (Dimensions: 2.5" x 3.5").
  • Photo file size should be between 10 KB and 200 KB.
  • Image background must be white colored.
