About NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)

NEET is an all India pre-medical entrance test. Apart from government and private institutes, those who want to acquire first aid qualifications abroad also have to take the NEET exam.

Qualifications required for NEET are undergraduate, postgraduate, etc.

NEET exam courses:

  1. MBBS (Undergraduate Medical)
  2. BDS (Dental)
  3. BAMS (AYUSH)
  4. BUMS, BHMS, etc.

Image conversion for NEET form

Generally two types of photo are required in NEET examination, as follows: 1. Passport photograph 2. Postcard size photo.

There are special guidelines for photo, signature and thumb impression in this exam. The guidelines are:

Photo

  • The photo size must be between 10 KB and 200 KB.
  • The photograph format should be JPG only.
  • Passport Photo: 2.5 x 3.5 inches with 72 DPI
  • Postcard Size Photo: 4.25 x 3.5 inches with 72 DPI

Signature

  • The signature size must be within 4 KB to 30 KB.
  • The signature file format should be JPG only.
  • Dimensions - 2.5 x 3.5 inches with 72 DPI

Thumb

  • The thumb size must be between 10 KB and 200 KB.
  • The file format should be JPG only.
  • Dimensions - 2.5 x 3.5 inches with 72 DPI

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

What is the full form of NEET?

+

The full form of NEET is "National Eligibility cum Entrance Test".

What is the pass mark for the NEET exam?

+

Pass Mark for NEET:

  • The passing marks for the general category were 720–138.
  • While the SC/ST/OBC category passing marks were 137–108.
  • For the general PwD category, it is 137–122.
  • And for SC/ST/OBC- PwD is 121–108.

What are the eligibility criteria for the NEET examination?

+
